Challenge

To create an outstanding app that resonates with target audience and develop an entertaining way to share your pictures with friends in form of jigsaw puzzle. Also, add levels of achievement to compare the results of your friends.

To create an outstanding app that resonates with target audience and develop an entertaining way to share your pictures.

Solution

JigIt app lets you send jigs to an unlimited number of people. Before sending, you can also edit your pictures with special stickers and filters. Moreover, you can add a “bomb” to make your Jig disappear.

Besel pro.activ
Story Travelling
FMCG

We took over the village of Zonhoven with Becel ProActiv, engaging the villagers in a 4 week cholesterol lowering activation program. With daily activities to educate, support and motivate them, 77% lowered their cholesterol.

The campaign was broadcasted every day in prime time, was followed by weekly magazines and reached more than 1 million Belgians on YouTube. The participating villagers are still active brand ambassadors.

Knorr Snackpot - Release the Flavour

Spice Agency released the flavour of tasty Knorr Snackpots during the national launch of new Knorr pasta & rice snacks. With an integrated approach combining sampling, talkable activations & social influencers, we invited thousands of millenials to try asnackpot and #livethebreak!

The campaign covered targeted sampling days in 8 student cities, close collaborations with student organizations across the country and an integrated partnership with eSports platform Kayzr, including the first ever gaming Student League hosted by Knorr Snackpot.

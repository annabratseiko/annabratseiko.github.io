OUR STORY

Story Travel Companion

The story of SPICE is a story about creating engaging stories and impactful personalized interactions with consumers, to grow brands and businesses sustainably.

PIONEERING

Spice trade has always been a job for pioneers. Brave men discovering new horizons returned with both failures and successes, but always with new experiences. They built on learnings to build an economy.

That’s why SPICE is a story of Startup Projects, pioneering and prototyping new business ideas with a can do mentality. Some will fail, some will succeed, but all will build expertise towards next endeavours.

PIONEERING

STORY TRAVELING

Hundreds of spices and thousands of spice combinations exist, making it the perfect tool for any chef to craft their own unique story combining their personality with great ingredients, resulting in an elegant yet impactful combination.

That’s why SPICE is also a story of growing brands and businesses through Impactful Consumer Engagement.

Impactful

Touching basic consumer senses with a creative approach to create a deep and lasting impression. The origin of spices not only lies in flavouring but also in preserving food.

SPICE does not do short term fixes, but thinks within a long term ambition and vision.

Consumer

Only consumers make the magic happen, they just need the right tools and a sparkle of personalised inspiration at the right place & time. No bigger impact than consumers co-creating the story; nobody dislikes homemade food.

SPICE is 100% consumer-centric, in the thinking and in the doing.

Engagement

People are constantly looking for experiences, but a once-off experience is like love at first sight becoming a faraway friend, consumer interaction should be repetitive to sustain. Consumers also don’t think in online and offline channels. Nobody only talks on facebook or only goes out with his best friends, so genuine consumer engagement can’t stay single-channel either.

SPICE believes in integrated two-way consumer engagement.

Spice trade has built incredible new trade networks and ultimately trading spices became spreading stories, as the stories and experiences from far away were brought close to the people, through the people.

Those stories continued to be told around the family dinner table, where every meal now had its own personality, crafted for and by the consumers with a unique mix of SPICE.
